About US


Welcome to Royal Restaurant, a unique dining experience in the heart of Fairfield, west Sydney. Our restaurant offers a wide selection of classic and modern cuisine, all made from locally sourced ingredients. Whether you are looking for a hearty breakfast, a satisfying lunch, or a delicious dinner, you will find something to suit your taste and budget at Royal Restaurant.

We are open everyday of the week from 11am to 11pm, the kitchen closes at 10pm and stop orders then. You are advised to book as we are very often out of seats. You can reserve from our Reservation Menu or you have our contact details in the section above.
